How it works

One platform, start to finish

BCOMMZ carries your event from setup to final report — and puts the full experience in every attendee's pocket.

01

Configure

Set up branding, ticket types, access levels, registration forms, roles, and programme content.

02

Publish

Launch the public event website with live registration, speakers, agenda, and sponsor content.

03

Register

Capture delegates, process payments, issue confirmations, and manage access codes automatically.

04

Operate
